# Foreign interference: how Parliament is fighting the threat to EU democracy

The world’s first comprehensive law on artificial intelligence (AI) protects fundamental rights in the EU and helps counter disinformation and manipulation by introducing labelling requirements for AI-generated content. Parliament also adopted new rules to defend journalists against abusive lawsuits
