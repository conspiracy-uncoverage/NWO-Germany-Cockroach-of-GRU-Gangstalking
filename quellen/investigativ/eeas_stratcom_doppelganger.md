# Pro-Kremlin disinformation - Doppelganger operation

As part of the monitoring and analysis for the European Parliament Elections 2024, the EEAS Stratcom has detected Foreign Information Manipulation and Interference (FIMI) activities by the Doppelganger/RRN Media operation. This operation actively promoted Russian narratives to disrupt and interfere
