# Russian hybrid threats: EU agrees first listings in response to destabilising activities against the EU, its Member States and partners

In addition, the Council listed African Initiative, a news agency involved in spreading Russian propaganda and disinformation on the African continent, its editor-in-chief and a Russian Federal Security Service (FSB) Officer involved in coordinated disinformation campaigns, both in Europe and Africa
